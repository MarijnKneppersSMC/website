Machine Learning has a lot of real world applications. Here, we will be looking at 2 uses. One that has a real world use and one that is fun.

The first thing we will be looking at is GPT-3 (Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3). GPT-3 was created by OpenAI and is the third generation of the GPT-n series. GPT-3 is a Machine Learning algorithm specifically made to be able to generate text although it is also capable of writing basic code. GPT-3 has become so good that it has become next to impossible to disguise between text written by GPT-3 and text written by humans. Outside of it's higher capability to generate text, the text that is produced is significantly less toxic then it's predecesors.
